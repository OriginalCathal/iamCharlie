I am Charlie, a Product Manager

About

Over the last few years, I have been involved in some very exciting products. I have been fortunate to drive products from conception to completion. I have achieved this through a deep understanding of the technical environment and the process involved in development. I consider myself a design focused product manager who enjoys exploring the relationship between technology and design.

Process

This rough framework outlines my process for developing products & experiences:

1. Research and gather current and past information.
2. Structure the setup and content of the product.
3. Concept plan and strategy.
4. Create, evaluate and iterate deliverables such as:
○ Personas
○ Site maps
○ Use cases and scenarios
○ User flow
○ Sketches and Infographics
